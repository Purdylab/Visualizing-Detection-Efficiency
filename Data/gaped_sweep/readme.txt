Each Igor file contains 3 measured waves and 2 calculated waves

	x:
	the micrometer reading on the position of the sweeping wire
	total power:
	QPD sum port reading
	peak power:
	The value of the peak of the spectrum generated by the QPD difference port.

	xw0:
	x/Beam waist at the detector
	snr:
	Linearized peak power / total power
